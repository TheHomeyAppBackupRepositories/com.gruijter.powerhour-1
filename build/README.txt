Get data from a Homey power/gas/water meter device, and show the usage / production per hour, per day, per month and per year.
This can be used for instance with the Enelogic and Youless app, the Plugwise Smile P1 app, the BeeClear app, the Solar Power app, the Homewizard app, or any other app that has a cumulative Power Meter capability, a cumulative Gas Meter capability, or a cumulative Water Meter capability.
